## Overview

Inventory Management System is a full-featured web application built for a local business to efficiently manage their inventory operations. The system provides a complete solution for tracking products, managing stock levels, processing sales, and generating detailed reports. Built with PHP 7 and utilizing the AdminLTE template, it offers a professional and user-friendly interface for daily business operations.
