## Overview

Artly is a creative mobile application designed to help artists maintain a consistent drawing practice through AI-generated prompts and gamification. Inspired by Duolingo's successful streak mechanics, Artly encourages users to draw daily, track their progress, and share their artwork with a supportive community.

## Key Features

### Daily Drawing Prompts

- AI-generated creative prompts to inspire daily artwork
- Themed challenges to explore different subjects and styles
- Reference images to help guide your creative process

### Streak System

- Track your consecutive days of drawing
- Visual progress indicators showing weekly completion
- Motivational messaging to maintain consistency
- Gamified experience that rewards daily practice

### Community Gallery

- Share your daily drawings with fellow artists
- Browse and appreciate others' interpretations of the same prompts
- Like and engage with community artwork
- Build connections with creative individuals

### Progress Tracking

- Visual timeline of your artistic journey
- See your improvement over time
- Maintain accountability through consistent practice

## Technical Implementation

The app is built with **Flutter** for cross-platform compatibility (iOS and Android), with **Dart** as the primary language. The backend infrastructure is powered by **Golang**, ensuring efficient API responses and scalability. Development tools include **Android Studio** and **Xcode** for native platform optimization.

## Design Philosophy

Artly focuses on making creativity a daily habit rather than an occasional pursuit. By removing the barrier of "what should I draw?", the app helps artists focus on the practice itself. The social aspect creates accountability and inspiration, while the streak mechanic taps into the same motivational psychology that makes language learning apps successful.

## Development Status

Currently in active development, Artly represents a collaboration between developers passionate about fostering creativity through technology.
